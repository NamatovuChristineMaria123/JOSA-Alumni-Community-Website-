About JOSA Alumni

The JOSA Alumni Network is a global community of accomplished professionals united by our shared experience at JOSA. Our mission is to foster lifelong connections, support professional development, and contribute to the continued excellence of our alma mater.

Since our founding in 1985, we've grown to become one of the most active alumni networks, with members in over 120 countries making an impact in business, technology, arts, and public service.
